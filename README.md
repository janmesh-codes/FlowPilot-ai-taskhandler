# 🚀 FlowPilot

> **AI That Helps You Finish, Not Just Remember.**

FlowPilot is an AI-powered productivity platform that acts as your **AI Chief of Staff**, helping students, professionals, and teams plan, prioritize, schedule, and complete tasks before deadlines are missed.

Unlike traditional productivity apps that only send reminders, FlowPilot proactively analyzes workloads, creates execution plans, adapts schedules, and guides users through every stage of task completion using a multi-agent AI system powered by Google Gemini.
